JawnRemote — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026 · Jawnston Inc.

JawnRemote ("the app") lets you use your phone as a mouse and keyboard for your own Windows PC over your local Wi-Fi network. This policy explains what data is and isn't involved.

What the app does with your data

  • No account, no cloud. The app connects directly to a server you run on your own PC, on your own local network. We do not operate servers that receive your activity, and we do not require sign-in.
  • Stored only on your device. The PCs you save (name, IP address, port, and PIN) and your settings are stored locally on your phone. They are not sent to us or to anyone else.
  • What you type and where you point goes only to the PC you're connected to. It is never sent to us.

Network & security

Connections stay on your local network and are protected by a PIN shown on the PC. The PC server only accepts connections from devices on your own network.

Ads & tracking

None. JawnRemote shows no ads. It contains no advertising SDK and no analytics, does not read your Advertising ID, and collects no usage or device data — there is nothing for us, or any third party, to receive.

Purchases

The app is free and currently offers no in-app purchases.

Permissions

The app uses only the Internet/network permission, to reach your PC on your local network. It does not request access to your location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.

Children

JawnRemote is a general-purpose utility and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above and post the new version at this URL.
