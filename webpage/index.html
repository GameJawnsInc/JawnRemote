JawnRemote

Your phone is the mouse & keyboard.

Control your Windows PC from your phone over Wi-Fi — a smooth trackpad, a full keyboard, scroll and clicks. No cables, no account. The replacement for UnifiedRemote that actually works on Windows 11.

What it does

Trackpad
Drag to move, tap to click, two fingers to right-click or scroll. Double-tap and drag to select.
Full keyboard
Type live to your PC, plus arrows, Esc, Tab, and Ctrl/Alt/Win shortcuts like Ctrl+C.
Finds your PC
Auto-discovers the server on your Wi-Fi — or just type the IP it shows you.
PIN-secured
A PIN pairs your phone to your PC, so nobody else on the network can take over.
Local & private
Talks straight to your PC on your own network. No cloud, no sign-in, nothing collected.
One-tap install
The PC server is a single installer — it even opens the Windows Firewall for you.

Quick start

Get going in 2 minutes

Step 1 — Install the PC server

  1. Download JawnRemote-Server-Setup.exe (button above) and run it.
  2. Click Yes on the one Windows prompt, then Next → Install → Finish.
  3. A small window opens showing your PC's address and a PIN. Leave it running.
The installer opens the firewall on all networks automatically — the thing that breaks other remotes on Windows 11.

Step 2 — Get the app

  1. Install JawnRemote from Google Play on your phone.
  2. Make sure the phone is on the same Wi-Fi as the PC.

Step 3 — Connect

  1. Open the app. Your PC appears under Discovered — tap it.
  2. Enter the PIN from the server window. That's it.
  3. (If it isn't found automatically, tap Add PC and type the IP shown on the PC.)
